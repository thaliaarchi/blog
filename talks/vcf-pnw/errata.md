# Errata for VCF PNW talk

I delivered the talk without speaker notes, so some mistakes slipped in.
Additionally, some details have been clarified in further research.

- The tape was cleaned with cyclomethicone, not "alcohol".
- The person who first booted the recovered UNIX V4 on a PDP-11/40 was Ashlin
  Inwood, not "Ian Ashlin".
- The Shaded Picture System was a commercial E&S product that may have been
  unrelated to the interactive graphics lab. Further research is needed here.
- The system used for the graphics lab after DEC DOS was RSX-11M, not RT-11.
